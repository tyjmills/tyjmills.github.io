Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

  • KSL
  • Website

KSL uses visual hierarchy by making the main article larger than the rest to draw you there. They then have a coupke medium size articles which are the next important ones they want you to read. Then in a small size are all the rest of the articles.

PARC: Alignment

  • Lowe's
  • Website

In Lowe's Website they make good use of alignment by making sure each category is aligned on all signs with the other columns. This makes the flow easy to follow and read.

White Space and Clean Design

  • Lifetime
  • Website

When looking at the Lifetime Website it makes great use of the white spce by spreading out all images and words on the screen. By empty space between the text images and categories it gives it makes it easy to view and not overwhelming.
