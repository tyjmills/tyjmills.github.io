Persona 1

Male age 50 with a Masters Degree. He works full time in the city. He commutes over 30 minutes to work daily. He needs to know what the weather is like and road conditions so that he can plan his commute properly. He will more than likely access this site from his phone at home and a computer at work.

Persona 2

Female age 25 with a Bachelors Degree. She has younger kids and is a stay at home mom. She enjoys running and taking her kids to the park. She will use her phone to check the current and upcoming conditions to plan her day and week.

Scenarios

  • Be able to find current traffic conditions
  • It will be accessed to find current weather conditions.
  • Hourly forecasts can be looked up and observed.
  • For people trying to plan events there will be a weekly forecast.
  • Severe weather warnings will be available and easy to find for those wanting to head outdoors.
